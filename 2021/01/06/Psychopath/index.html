如何不喜欢一个人
Psychopath Free

YEAR:2021


亚马逊心理学畅销图书《如何不喜欢一个人》。作者：杰克森 · 麦肯锡 曾与“毒型人格”者交往过，是 PsychopathFree.com 的创始人之一，这个在线情感互助支持平台每个月都能对数以百万计的情感虐待幸存者伸出援手。摆脱和心理变态的人之间的纠缠，对自我心灵的认知，这必定是一段阴暗的旅程。疗愈过去、重拾尊严、找回自我，重新建立自我判断与底线，找回面对末来的勇气。
创作之时我放弃了以性别题材做表现，而是选择了用乌云与日的天气形象。感情犹如天上云，阴晴不定，在阴云之后的放晴天，阳光往往会格外温暖，拨开阴云及见日。


Amazon best selling psychology books Psychopath Free. Jackson MacKenzie is the co-founder of PsychopathFree.com, an online support community that reaches millions of abuse survivors each month.Written from the heart, Psychopath Free is the first guide for survivors written by a survivor, offering hope for healing and thriving after psychopathic abuse. Say goodbye to the chaos, self-doubt, and victimization. You are free.
At the time of creation, I gave up the gender theme for expression and chose to use the weather image of dark clouds and sun. Emotions are like the clouds in the sky, which are cloudy and uncertain, and the sun tends to be warmer on the sunny days after the cloudy ones.
