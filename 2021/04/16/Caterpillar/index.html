好饿的毛毛虫
The Very Hungry Caterpillar

YEAR:2021


一只刚出生的小毛毛虫，拖着饿扁的肚皮，一步一步往前爬，锲而不舍地找东西吃。星期一吃了一个苹果；星期二吃了两个梨子……毛毛虫愈变愈大，然后它躲进茧里，猜猜看，它会变成什么？我尝试对这本书籍的封面进行了重绘。


A newly hatched caterpillar, dragging its hungry belly, crawled forward step by step, tirelessly searching for something to eat. On Monday, it ate an apple; on Tuesday, it ate two pears... The caterpillar grew larger and larger, until it finally hid itself inside a cocoon. Guess what it will become? I attempted to repaint the cover of this book.
