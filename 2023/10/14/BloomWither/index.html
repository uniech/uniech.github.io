一朵花的告白
Bloom & Wither

YEAR:2023


花开终会凋零，那它意义在哪？死亡不是终结，而是另一场出发。正因为花开终会凋零，花开才有了意义。一朵永远都不会凋谢的花，一朵毫无生命力的花，一朵永远都没有变化，没有生长，也没有绽放的花，真的会觉得它有意思吗？花了点时间为我曾经的一张海报做了改变，刚好契合了我想表达的寓意。


Flowers eventually wither, but where does their significance lie? Death is not the end, but another beginning. It's precisely because flowers will eventually wither that their blooming holds meaning. Would a flower that never withers, a flower lacking vitality, or a flower that remains unchanged, neither growing nor blossoming, truly be interesting? I spent some time making changes to a poster I once created, and it happened to align perfectly with the message I wanted to convey.
