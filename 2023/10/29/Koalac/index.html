考拉交流
KOALA.C
YEAR:2023

无意间在网上看到一个设计师和客户聊天中谈到的 logo 需求（虽然可能只是一种吸引流量的方式）但我感觉有点意思就尝试了一下。我以考拉经常睡觉的样子结合了信息气泡，其中气泡的形象是从字母 O 演变过来的，作为一个外语交流平台类型的虚拟作品练习。

I accidentally came across a logo request on the internet that a designer talked about with a client (although it may just be a way to attract traffic), but I felt it was interesting so I gave it a try. I combined a message bubble with the image of a koala that sleeps a lot, with the image of the bubble evolving from the letter O, as a virtual work for a foreign language exchange platform.
