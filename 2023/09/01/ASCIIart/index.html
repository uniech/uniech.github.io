消失的字符艺术
Disappear ASCII

YEAR:2023


ASCII 图案，又称“文字图”、“字符画”，是一种主要依靠电脑 ASCII 字符来表达图像的艺术形式。用于当文字比图像更稳定和更快显示的场合，随着图形浏览越来越流行，导致 ASCII 艺术的衰落。


ASCII graphics, also known as "word pictures" and "character drawings", is an art form that relies heavily on computerized ASCII characters to express an image. Used in situations where text is more stable and faster to display than images, ASCII art has declined as graphical browsing has become more popular.
