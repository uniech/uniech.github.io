安静 和 安静了
Silence
YEAR:2023

周杰伦和 Selina 联手创作的《安静了》这首歌，是周杰伦邀请 S.H.E 参观他的新公司时，当场写出来的。S.H.E《Play》专辑里面的一首歌曲《借口》拼贴了周杰伦的许多歌名，周杰伦听到后就表示，给别人拼不如自己拼给 S.H.E。周杰伦也实现诺言，自己拼贴自己的原唱歌曲《安静》，完成了这首曲子。而周杰伦写完旋律录制 DEMO 时要求 Selina 亲自填词，因此 Selina 将自己的恋爱经验写出《安静了》的歌词。在安静中营造一种破碎的氛围，很奇妙的感觉。

The song "Quieter" is a collaborative work between Jay Chou and Selina. It was written on the spot when Jay Chou invited S.H.E to visit his new company. One of the songs from S.H.E's album "Play," titled "Excuses," included a collage of many of Jay Chou's song titles. When Jay heard it, he expressed that it would be better to create a collage for someone else than for S.H.E. Jay fulfilled his promise and completed the song by collaging his own original song "Quieter." After Jay wrote the melody and recorded the demo, he requested Selina to personally write the lyrics. As a result, Selina wrote the lyrics of "Quieter" based on her own relationship experience.It's wonderful to create a broken atmosphere in the quiet.
