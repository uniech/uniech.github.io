安静 安静了
Silence It's Quiet Now

YEAR:2023


周杰伦和 Selina 联手创作的《安静了》这首歌，是周杰伦邀请 S.H.E 参观他的新公司时，当场写出来的。S.H.E《Play》专辑里面的一首歌曲《借口》拼贴了周杰伦的许多歌名，周杰伦听到后就表示，给别人拼不如自己拼给 S.H.E。周杰伦也实现诺言，自己拼贴自己的原唱歌曲《安静》，完成了这首曲子。而周杰伦写完旋律录制 DEMO 时要求 Selina 亲自填词，因此 Selina 将自己的恋爱经验写出《安静了》的歌词。我在一次随机播放音乐时，这两首歌被奇妙的连接在了一起。


The song "It's Quiet Now," a collaborative creation by Jay Chou and Selina, was spontaneously composed when Jay Chou invited S.H.E to visit his new company. Upon hearing S.H.E's song "Excuse" from the "Play" album, which incorporated many of Jay Chou's song titles, Jay Chou expressed that he preferred to create a piece for S.H.E himself. Keeping his promise, Jay Chou pieced together his original song "Silence" for them. When Jay Chou finished composing the melody and recording the demo, he requested Selina to personally write the lyrics. Thus, Selina drew from her own romantic experiences to craft the lyrics for "It's Quiet Now." Interestingly, during a random music playback session, these two songs were magically linked together.
