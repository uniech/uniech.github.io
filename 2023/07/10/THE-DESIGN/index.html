THE DESIGN FONT MOUCK

本性善良的人都晚熟，并且是被“劣人”催熟的，后来虽然开窍了，但也仍然善良与赤诚。 当别人聪明伶俐时，他们又傻又呆；当别人心机用尽渐入颓境时，他们恰好灵魂开窍。后来虽然开窍了，但也仍然善良与赤诚，不断的寻找同类，最后却成了最孤独的一个。
大丈夫立于天地间，岂能郁郁久居人下。

Good natured people are late maturing, and is “bad people” to ripen, later although the enlightenment, but also still good and sincere. When others are clever and clever, they are stupid and dumb; when others are exhausted and gradually into decadence, they happen to be enlightened soul. Later, although the enlightenment, but also still kind and sincere, constantly looking for the same kind, but finally became the loneliest one.
