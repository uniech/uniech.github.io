即视感
Déjà vu

YEAR:2024


有时候会突然感觉眼前的事物或者场景似曾相识，貌似在何时接触过的熟悉感，如果发生的场景有其他人存在，甚至能够清晰的预感他人接下来要做出的行为。


Sometimes, there's a sudden feeling of déjà vu, where the things or scenes in front of you seem oddly familiar, as if you've encountered them before. This sense of familiarity can evoke memories of when or where you might have experienced them previously. If there are other people present in the scene, you may even have a clear premonition of what actions they're about to take.
