陈年旧物
Old Thing

YEAR:2024


过年回家之后的某一天，我在洗完脸之后盯着那个不知道已经用了多久的脸盆发呆，上面的图案都是被时间水流侵蚀的痕迹，可能已经快要认不清了。我记下了这个搪瓷盆和卫生间上瓷砖的图案，它们好像理所应当的在这个房子里一直待了很久。


One day after returning home for the Chinese New Year, I found myself staring blankly at the basin in the bathroom after washing my face. The patterns on it had been eroded by the passage of time, and they were becoming increasingly difficult to recognize. I made a mental note of these patterns, along with those on the tiles in the bathroom. They seemed to have naturally belonged in this house for so long.
