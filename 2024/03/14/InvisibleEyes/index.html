假如看不见它们的眼睛
Invisible Eyes

YEAR:2024


流浪动物在多年后被主人找回之时，动物身上长期积累的尖酸都能被主人所了解，这是为什么呢？明明它们并不会说话。通过肢体语言吗？但很多时候我们并不理解动物肢体的含义，还是说因为眼睛？这种习以为常的感官却是情绪最直观的表现，人类并不理解动物在想什么，但依旧能够通过眼睛明白它们此刻的情绪，这种眼神的情绪表达并不仅仅只出现在亲近人类的猫狗身上，那假如它们没有眼睛呢？


When stray animals are found by their owners after many years, their accumulated bitterness can still be understood by the owners, even though the animals cannot speak. Why is this so? Clearly, they don't communicate verbally. Is it through body language? But often, we don't fully understand the meaning of animal gestures. Or is it because of their eyes? This seemingly ordinary sense is, in fact, the most direct expression of emotions. Humans may not understand what animals are thinking, but they can still perceive their current emotions through their eyes. This emotional expression through eyes is not limited to cats and dogs that are close to humans. But what if they didn't have eyes?
