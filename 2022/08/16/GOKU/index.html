悟空儿童运动馆
GOKU KIDSGYM

YEAR:2022


GOKU 悟空儿童运动馆是合肥乐体体育旗下品牌，全国连锁儿童运动训练机构。中国儿童体适能联盟会员单位，是安徽首家儿童体适能训练课程创办机构，以孙悟空这个家喻户晓的形象闯入了人们的视线。针对 2-12 岁儿童，以儿童身体生长发育基本规律为基础，致力于帮助孩子掌握科学的运动方法，建立正确的运动技能，养成良好的运动习惯，从而实现孩子在身体、心理、智力三方面的全面发展。为了品牌更好的发展进行了一次围绕三原色的视觉升级。


GOKU KIDSGYM, a brand under Hefei Leti Sports, is a nationwide chain of children's sports training institutions. As a member of the China Children's Fitness Alliance, it is the first institution in Anhui to offer children's fitness training courses. Featuring the well-known image of Sun Wukong (Monkey King), it has captured people's attention. Targeting children aged 2-12, the gym is based on the basic laws of children's physical growth and development, aiming to help children master scientific sports methods, establish correct sports skills, and develop good exercise habits, thereby achieving comprehensive development in physical, psychological, and intellectual aspects. To enhance the brand's development, a visual upgrade centered around the three primary colors was conducted.
