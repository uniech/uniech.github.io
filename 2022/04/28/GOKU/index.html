悟空儿童运动馆
GOKU KIDSGYM
YEAR:2022

GOKU 悟空儿童运动馆是合肥乐体体育旗下品牌，全国连锁儿童运动训练机构。中国儿童体适能联盟会员单位，是安徽首家儿童体适能训练课程创办机构，以孙悟空这个家喻户晓的形象闯入了人们的视线。针对 2-12 岁儿童，以儿童身体生长发育基本规律为基础，致力于帮助孩子掌握科学的运动方法，建立正确的运动技能，养成良好的运动习惯，从而实现孩子在身体、心理、智力三方面的全面发展。为了更好的发展而迎来了一次品牌升级，和全方位的视觉升级应用延展。

GOKU KIDSGYM is a brand under LetiTiyu in Hefei and is a nationwide chain of children's sports training institutions. It is a member of the China Children's Physical Fitness Alliance and the first institution in Anhui to establish children's physical fitness training courses. By leveraging the widely recognized image of Sun Wukong, it has caught people's attention. Targeting children aged 2-12, the center is committed to helping children grasp scientific exercise methods, develop proper movement skills, cultivate good exercise habits, and achieve comprehensive development in terms of physical, psychological, and intellectual aspects. In order to facilitate better development, the brand has undergone a brand upgrade and comprehensive visual enhancements.
